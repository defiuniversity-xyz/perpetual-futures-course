## üí° Key Principles

1. **Risk First**: Understanding liquidation mechanics and margin requirements protects your capital. Never risk more than you can afford to lose.

2. **Architecture Matters**: CLOB protocols offer CEX-like experience. Oracle pools offer zero slippage but carry different risks. Choose based on your strategy.

3. **Start Small**: Begin with low leverage and isolated margin. Master basics before advanced strategies.

4. **Funding Rates Matter**: High funding rates can erode profits. Always check before opening positions.

5. **Multi-Protocol**: Different protocols excel at different things. Learn multiple platforms to optimize your strategies.
